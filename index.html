About Me

I’m Gabriel Edet — a web designer and developer who turns ideas into clean, functional, and visually compelling digital experiences. I design with purpose, blending thoughtful aesthetics with solid engineering to create websites that are intuitive, engaging, and built to last. My journey into web design began at the intersection of creativity and technology, and over time I’ve refined my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern web practices. Beyond code, I draw inspiration from art, photography, and writing, influences that shape how I approach layout, typography, and user experience. I believe great design should not only look good, but feel effortless to use. My goal is simple: to make the digital world more beautiful and accessible—one website at a time. If you’re looking to bring a vision to life with clarity and intention, let’s work together.
